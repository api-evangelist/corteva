# Corteva (corteva)

Corteva Agriscience is a publicly traded, Fortune 500 global pure-play agriculture company that provides farmers around the world with one of the most complete portfolios in the industry, including a balanced and diverse mix of seed, crop protection, and digital products. Corteva was formed as the spinoff of the agriculture division of DowDuPont in June 2019. The company markets seeds under the Pioneer, Brevant, and other brands and crop protection products including herbicides, fungicides, insecticides, and biologicals. Corteva also operates Granular, a farm management software platform.
